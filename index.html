About Me

Hello! I'm Prabin Roka, a dedicated student, learner, and explorer from Nepal—a country known for its breathtaking landscapes and rich cultural heritage.


My academic journey led me to the College of Idaho, where I am double majoring in Computer Science Mathematics, and Digital Marketing, alongside a minor in Arts. My passion lies at the intersection of technology and language, and I am particularly interested in Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing.


One of my long-term aspirations is to contribute to linguistic diversity in AI. While most language models primarily cater to widely spoken languages like English, I aim to develop a language model for Nepali. By doing so, I hope to enhance the digital representation of the Nepali language and culture, making AI more inclusive and accessible for Nepali speakers worldwide.


With a strong foundation in programming, data analysis, and machine learning, I am eager to explore new challenges, collaborate on meaningful projects, and continue learning in this ever-evolving field.
